A little about me


Flashback - I’m five years old barreling down the staircase in a laundry basket. I’m curious to know if there’s a way to make the experience of getting down the stairs more fun. Not one of my brightest ideas because seconds later I was met with the wall’s stiff embrace. Fast-forward a few years and some “how did I get here moments,” my skills at anticipating potential outcomes has improved, but my curiosity has only grown.

My journey towards design has taken many paths, but curiosity has been the constant spark in our relationship. It encouraged me to learn new technologies, like customizing my Xanga at age eleven. It drove my passion for storytelling while pursuing a degree in political science. It fostered a strategic mindset to think both big and small when learning the ins and outs of product management. My experiences have allowed me to understand the world as an interconnected web of people, ideas, and communication.

As a UX Designer I am an advocate for people & ideas, I am a curious-soul searching for answers, I am an architect of human experiences.

I have an ever-growing bucket-list to keep my life exciting. Most notable accomplishments being:

  • Donating more than a gallon of blood
  • Bungee Jumping off the Bridge to Nowhere
