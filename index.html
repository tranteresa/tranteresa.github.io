A little about me



My curiosity to understand the world has been the constant spark in my relationship towards design. It encouraged me to learn new technologies, like customizing my Xanga at age eleven (and thankfully giving me the will to persevere through the age of dial-up internet). It drove my passion for storytelling while pursuing a degree in political science. It fostered a strategic mindset to think both big and small in my role as a product manager.

As a UX Designer, I am a combination of my experiences. I am an advocate for multiple perspectives, I am a curious-soul who loves to breakdown and understand ideas, I am an architect of human experiences and curiosity is my inspiration.

I have an ever-growing bucket-list to keep my life exciting. Most notable accomplishments are donating more than a gallon of blood (not all at once, haha) and bungee jumping off the Bridge to Nowhere.

I’m seeking a talented team who is passionate about the products they build and experiences they create. I’d love a team that can stir my curiosity and challenge my weaknesses. Let's connect! Check out my resume below for more details or drop me a line at hello@teresatran.co.
