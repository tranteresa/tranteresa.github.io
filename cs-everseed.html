The Overview
What is Everseed?


Everseed is an educational goals-based concept project that encourages users to practice low-carbon habits. I developed a low-fidelity mobile prototype to guide the design decisions for a responsive product showcase.


The Challenge
I want to help!

In 2017, the United States experienced not one, not two, but three major hurricanes that had devastating affects. Scientist have linked warming global temperatures to the increase of hurricanes and other natural disasters.

Inspiration by the growing concern for climate change, I observed that people wanted to help but didn't know how. This design challenge empowers users to take action in reducing their carbon footprint.

The Solution
Doing what's easy and what's right

I wanted to design an app that influenced behavior and created change on a global scale. How can we use existing technology to affect climate change? While users' primary interaction would be through their mobile devices, I focused on developing the product showcase as this would be users' first interaction with Everseed. My goal was to creating a meaningful experience that not only informed, but engaged users.

Users identified money, social accountability, and education as three key motivators. I designed the product showcase to incorporate these motivators to sustain user engagement and improve user experience.
