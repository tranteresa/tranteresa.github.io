A little more about me




Flashback - I’m five years old barreling down the staircase in a laundry basket. I’m curious to know if there’s a way to make the experience of getting down the stairs faster and more fun. Not one of my brightest ideas because seconds later I was met with the wall’s stiff embrace. Fast-forward a few years and plenty of “how did I get here moments,” my curiosity to understand the world has been the constant spark in my journey towards design.

It encouraged me to learn new technologies, like customizing my Xanga at age eleven (and thankfully giving me the will to persevere through the age of dial-up internet). It drove my passion for storytelling while pursuing a degree in political science. It fostered a strategic mindset to think both big and small in my role as a product manager.

As a UX Designer, I am a combination of my experiences. I am an advocate for multiple perspectives, I am an explorer traversing the paths of new ideas, I am an architect of human experiences and curiosity is my inspiration.

I have an ever-growing bucket-list to keep my life exciting. Most notable accomplishments are donating more than a gallon of blood (not all at once, haha) and bungee jumping off the Bridge to Nowhere.
